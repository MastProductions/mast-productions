About

Founded in 2015, this video production and editing company is dedicated to telling compelling, meaningful stories through visual media. From day one, our mission has been to create content that not only captures attention but also inspires action and builds community.

Over the years, we've grown from a solo initiative into a trusted partner for nonprofits, artists, and businesses across Europe. We specialize in crafting short-form social media content, cultural storytelling, campaign videos, and digital features that communicate ideas clearly and creatively.

Led by founder Alice Järvet, a professional with a background in international diplomacy, economics, and public service, we blend creativity with strategy. Our work supports community empowerment, social innovation, and cross-cultural connection — values that are central to our vision.

Alice is also a board member of UKUfest, Hands for Ukraine, and the Chambre de Commerce et d'Industrie France Estonie (CCIFE), and a multilingual communicator fluent in Estonian, English, French, Finnish, and Russian. This unique blend of skills allows us to serve a wide range of clients and causes with clarity, empathy, and professional excellence.

Services

Whether you're a nonprofit, artist, activist, or business owner, MAST Productions offers tailored video solutions that make your story stand out.

Basic Package

  • Up to 1-minute edited video (ideal for social media platforms)
  • We can shoot or use client-provided footage
  • Basic text overlays, transitions, and green screen recordings
  • Color correction + audio normalization
  • 1 round of revisions

Pro Package

  • Up to 3-minute edited video (for campaigns, interviews, promos)
  • Pre-edit consultation + storyboard guidance
  • Includes sound design, music licensing, and motion graphics
  • 2 rounds of revisions
  • Output in formats for social media, YouTube, or websites

Premium Package

  • Up to 60-minute full-feature video or video series (documentary, event, or story)
  • Full production support: filming, direction, scripting
  • Professional editing with animations, subtitles, and branding
  • Voiceover & translation options
  • Up to 3 revisions + social media cuts

À La Carte Options

  • Subtitling and translation (Estonian, English, French, Russian, Finnish)
  • Short-form content repurposing
  • Consulting on video strategy and storytelling
  • Event coverage (on request)

Contact me to discuss a custom package or collaboration idea that fits your goals.
