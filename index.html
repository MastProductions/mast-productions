About Me

Alice Järvet – Founder of MAST Productions

MAST Productions was founded in 2015 by Alice Järvet. With over a decade of experience in video production and editing, MAST Productions creates compelling visual content that communicates stories across cultures and communities.

Our mission is to produce content that not only captivates but inspires and informs. Whether you're an NGO, artist, business, or cultural festival – our goal is to amplify your voice through visual storytelling.

Service Packages

Video production tailored to your story

Basic Package

  • Up to 1-minute edited video (ideal for social media platforms)
  • We can shoot or use client-provided footage
  • Basic text overlays, transitions, green screen recordings
  • Color correction + audio normalisation
  • 1 round of revisions

Pro Package

  • Up to 3-minute edited video (for campaigns, interviews, promos)
  • Pre-edit consultation + storyboard guidance
  • Includes sound design, music licensing, motion graphics
  • 2 rounds of revisions
  • Output in formats for social media, YouTube, or websites

Premium Package

  • Up to 60-minute full-feature video or series (documentary, event, story)
  • Full production: filming, direction, scripting
  • Professional editing, animations, subtitles, branding
  • Voiceover & translation options
  • Up to 3 revisions + social media cuts

À La Carte Options

  • Subtitling and translation (Estonian, English, French, Russian, Finnish)
  • Short-form content repurposing
  • Consulting on video strategy and storytelling
  • Event coverage (on request)
