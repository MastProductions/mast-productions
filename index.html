    About Me

    Alice Järvet – Founder of MAST Productions

    MAST Productions was founded in 2015 by Alice Järvet. With over a decade of experience in video production and editing, MAST Productions creates compelling visual content that communicates stories across cultures and communities.

    Our mission is to produce content that not only captivates but inspires and informs. Whether you're an NGO, artist, business, or cultural festival – our goal is to amplify your message with meaningful media.

    Service Packages

    Video production tailored to your story

    Basic Package

    Up to 1-minute edited video (ideal for social media platforms). We can shoot or use client-provided footage. Basic text overlays, transitions, green screen recordings, color correction + audio normalization. 1 round of revisions.

    Pro Package

    Up to 3-minute edited video (for campaigns, interviews, promos). Includes pre-edit consultation, storyboard guidance, sound design, music licensing, and motion graphics. 2 rounds of revisions. Formats optimized for all platforms.

    Premium Package

    Up to 60-minute full-feature video or series. Full production: filming, direction, scripting, editing, animations, subtitles, branding, voiceover & translation. Up to 3 revisions + social media cuts.

    À La Carte Options

    Subtitling and translation (Estonian, English, French, Russian, Finnish), short-form content repurposing, storytelling consulting, event coverage (on request).
